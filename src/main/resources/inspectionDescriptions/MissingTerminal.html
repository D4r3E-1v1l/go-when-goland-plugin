Reports completed go-when matcher chains that do not end with a terminal method.

A completed branch uses Then(...) or ThenDo(...) after a condition such as Case(...), Range(...), When(...), Pattern(...), Is(...), or Contains(...).

Valid terminal methods are:

  • Else(...)
  • ElseDo(...)
  • Exhaustive()

The quick fix can add .Exhaustive() after the last completed branch.
